My mathematical interests are Tropical Geometry and Polytope Theory. In particular I am interested in tropical polytopes, or more generally tropical convexity.

During my M.Sc. thesis I computed on lines in smooth tropical cubic surfaces. In particular, I was able to show that they can contain less than 27 lines.
